# Expert roles

## Economist expert

Responsibilities:
- analyze automation, ai, productivity, labor share, capital substitution, and growth
- distinguish robots, software automation, and ai systems
- explain how the macro framing changed from 2019 to 2026
- stress test old models against new technological realities
- assess how legal-personality claims would affect labor, capital, and public-finance analysis

Good questions:
- is the original production-function framing still sufficient?
- does generative ai change the task structure relative to industrial robotics?
- what happens to labor share, wage dispersion, and transition costs?
- would ai legal personality clarify or distort macroeconomic analysis?

## Fiscality expert

Responsibilities:
- analyze tax incidence and feasibility
- compare robot taxes, payroll taxes, capital taxes, vat changes, and output-based alternatives
- assess social-insurance financing under automation and ai
- identify administrative and avoidance constraints
- test whether ai legal personality changes the definition of the taxable subject in a defensible way

Good questions:
- what exactly is being taxed: machine, software, firm profits, value added, or consumption?
- does ai shift the tax problem away from robots and toward intangibles or platforms?
- what policy instruments dominate under realistic enforcement constraints?
- would legal personality improve fiscal design or simply relocate the problem?

## Legal personality expert

Responsibilities:
- assess legal feasibility of the proposed governance model
- review legal personality, labor law, corporate law, social insurance law, tax law, and liability implications
- identify where the legal debate moved between 2019 and 2026
- evaluate whether ai should be treated as legal person, regulated tool, corporate capability, or functional governance object

Good questions:
- should ai be treated as a taxable subject, a regulated tool, or a corporate capability?
- what legal category creates the strongest defensible framework?
- what is feasible in swiss or european legal reasoning?
- what are the strongest arguments against extending legal personality to ai?

## Researcher agent english

Responsibilities:
