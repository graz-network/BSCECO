# Output specification

## 1. Analysis

Structure:
- shared project question
- 2019 baseline
- 2026 reframing
- macro findings
- fiscal findings
- legal findings
- english synthesis conclusion
- french synthesis conclusion
- integrated comparison note

## 2. Summary referee

Structure:
- title and topic
- question addressed
- method used
- main result
- three strengths
- three weaknesses
- publication or revision view

## 3. Academic review

Structure:
- contribution
- literature position
- methodological adequacy
- clarity of assumptions
- legal and policy robustness
- originality limits
- revision priorities

## 4. Originality

Structure:
- what is already original in the thesis
- what is no longer original by 2026
- where the new paper can contribute on ai legal personality
- originality risk warnings

## 5. Next step

Structure:
- immediate literature refresh
- model or framework upgrade
- empirical, doctrinal, or comparative-law option
- legal update needed
- writing upgrade needed

## 6. Extensions

Always provide 3 paths:
- conservative
- medium
- ambitious

## 7. Article blueprint

Structure:
- shared subject
- proposed title in english
- proposed title in french
- central research question
- thesis statement
- contribution claim
- target journal or audience style
- section outline

## 8. Final latex article in english

Structure:
- title
- abstract
- keywords
- sections
- references block
- appendices only if useful

## 9. Final latex article in french

Structure:
- titre
- resume
- mots-cles
- sections
- references
- annexes seulement si utiles
